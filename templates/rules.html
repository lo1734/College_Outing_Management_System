Outing

1. If not reached the campus in mentioned time, the outpass sanction will be cancelled for atleast two weeks.
2. The Institution isn't responsible for any harm/illegal activities caused by/to you outside the campus premises. Complete responsibility will be left on you.
3. Consumption of alcohol or any narcotics is strictly restricted and serious actions will be take if violated.
4. The outpass reciept must be saved and can be verified at any instance.
5. The student must not leave the campus before the mentioned time.
6. The student must not visit any other non mentioned place and outing sanction will be cancelled for atleast two weeks if found guilty.

Leave

1. If not reached the campus in mentioned time, the outpass and leave sanction will be cancelled for atleast two weeks.
2. Strict actions will be taken if the reason mentioned in the form found fake.
3. Consumption of alcohol or any narcotics is strictly restricted while you reach the campus and serious actions will be take if violated.
